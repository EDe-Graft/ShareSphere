SLIDE 3: GOAL OF RESEARCH/PROJECT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Goal of Research/Project

Content:
Develop a full-stack web platform that enables students to share resources 
(books, clothing, furniture, and miscellaneous items) to:

• Reduce waste and promote sustainability
• Help students save money
• Build stronger campus community connections
• Create a circular economy within educational institutions

================================================================================

SLIDE 4: ABSTRACT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Abstract

Content:
ShareSphere addresses student sustainability and affordability challenges by 
enabling seamless resource sharing across books, clothing, furniture, and 
miscellaneous items. This full-stack web platform facilitates community-driven 
exchanges, helping students reduce waste, save money, and build stronger 
campus connections.

================================================================================

SLIDE 5: INTRODUCTION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Introduction

Content:
• Problem: Students face high costs and waste generation
• Solution: Peer-to-peer resource sharing platform
• Target Users: College and university students
• Key Benefits:
  • Economic: Reduce student expenses
  • Environmental: Minimize waste through reuse
  • Social: Build campus community connections

================================================================================

SLIDE 6: METHODOLOGY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Title: Methodology

Content:
Technology Stack Selection:
• Frontend: React.js, Vite, TailwindCSS, shadcn/ui
• Backend: Node.js, Express.js, PostgreSQL
• Authentication: Passport.js (Google OAuth, GitHub)
• Storage: Cloudinary for image management
• Email: Nodemailer for notifications

Development Approach:
• Agile development methodology
• RESTful API design
• Responsive, mobile-first design
